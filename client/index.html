Building Creative Minds With Dance & Music



Created with tiny dancers in mind, we offer exciting classes in creative movement, ballet, tap, storytime theatre, and preschool hiphop. Classes are designed for students from 2 to 10 years old.

Brand new friendships are made, and children have the ideal environment for exploring their imagination, grow their minds, and let out their energy! Dancing can help encourage social interation, physical activity, improved self-esteem, and education. Classes are taught by a certified instructor. Working with all child care, elementary, genders, and abilities.
